Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: 2026-07-15 · Magies Technology

English

MagiesTerminal (“the App”) is a desktop SSH / SFTP / terminal workspace. This policy describes what we collect through the App and the website at shell.magies.top (“the Site”).

What we collect

  • Site analytics (optional operational logs): the Site may record aggregate visit and download events (for example path, approximate region, user-agent, session id) to understand product adoption. These are not used to build advertising profiles.
  • Contact email: if you email us (for example via the in-app or Site contact action), we process the message content you send in order to respond.
  • App local data: hosts, credentials, keys, settings, and chat history stay on your device (and any sync destination you configure). We do not operate a mandatory cloud account for core App features.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not require you to create a Magies account to download or use the App.
  • Third-party AI providers only receive data when you configure an API key and send requests.

Third parties

Downloads may be served via our CDN / mirror infrastructure. Analytics and hosting providers process technical logs under their own terms. AI model providers process prompts when you use Magies Agent with your own API keys.

中文

MagiesTerminal（「本应用」）是桌面端 SSH / SFTP / 终端工作空间。本政策说明我们通过本应用与 shell.magies.top（「本网站」）处理哪些信息。

我们收集什么

  • 网站运营数据：网站可能记录聚合的访问与下载事件（路径、大致地区、UA、会话 ID 等），用于了解产品使用情况，不作广告画像。
  • 联系邮箱：若你通过应用内或网站「问题咨询」等方式发信，我们会处理你发送的内容以便回复。
  • 应用本地数据：主机、凭据、密钥、设置与对话记录保存在你的设备（及你自行配置的同步目标）上。核心功能不强制注册云账号。

我们不会做什么

  • 不会出售个人数据。
  • 下载与使用本应用无需强制创建 Magies 账号。
  • 仅当你配置 API Key 并主动请求时，第三方 AI 供应商才会收到相关数据。

第三方

下载可能经由 CDN / 镜像分发。托管与统计服务会处理技术日志。使用 Magies Agent 时，模型供应商按你配置的 Key 处理请求内容。
